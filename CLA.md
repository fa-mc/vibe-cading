# vibe-cading Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

**Status: DRAFT — pending counsel review. Not yet in effect.**

Thank you for contributing to `vibe-cading`. To keep the project able to offer
both its open-source (AGPLv3) edition **and** commercial licenses to parties who
cannot accept AGPLv3 terms, we ask every contributor to agree to this CLA.

This Agreement is between you ("**You**") and the `vibe-cading` project
maintainer ("**the Maintainer**"). It applies to every Contribution You submit.

## 1. Definitions

- **"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship — code,
  documentation, configuration, or other material — that You submit to the
  project (e.g. by pull request, patch, or commit).
- **"Submit"** means any form of communication sent to the project's
  repositories or maintainers for inclusion in the project.

## 2. You retain your copyright

You **keep full copyright ownership** of Your Contributions. **This CLA does
NOT assign copyright** to the Maintainer. It grants the licenses in §3–§4 below
so the project can use and license Your Contribution as part of `vibe-cading`.

## 3. Copyright license grant (the relicensing grant)

You grant the Maintainer a perpetual, worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free,
irrevocable copyright license to reproduce, prepare derivative works of,
publicly display, publicly perform, sublicense, and distribute Your
Contributions and derivative works thereof, **under any license terms the
Maintainer chooses — including both the AGPLv3 (or a later version) and
separate proprietary / commercial license terms.**

This is the clause that lets the Maintainer offer the commercial-license
edition (Tier 2) over an engine that includes Your Contribution.

## 4. Patent license grant

You grant the Maintainer and recipients of `vibe-cading` a perpetual,
worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free, irrevocable (except as stated below)
patent license to make, have made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and
otherwise transfer Your Contribution, where such license applies only to those
patent claims licensable by You that are necessarily infringed by Your
Contribution alone or by combination of Your Contribution with the project.
If any entity institutes patent litigation alleging that the project or a
Contribution constitutes patent infringement, the patent licenses granted
under this CLA for that Contribution terminate as of the date such litigation
is filed. `[COUNSEL: confirm patent-grant scope + defensive-termination wording.]`

## 5. The project stays open (contributor assurance)

The Maintainer commits to continue making Your Contribution available to the
public under AGPLv3 (or a later AGPL version). The commercial-license rights in
§3 are **additional** to — not a replacement for — the open-source release. The
relicensing grant does not permit removing the public AGPLv3 edition.

## 6. Your representations

You represent that:
1. Each Contribution is Your original creation, or You have the right to submit
   it under this CLA.
2. If Your employer has rights to intellectual property You create, You have
   received permission to make the Contribution on behalf of that employer, or
   the employer has waived such rights.
3. Your Contribution does not, to Your knowledge, violate any third-party
   rights.

## 7. No obligation

The Maintainer is not obligated to use or include Your Contribution.

Except as stated above, Your Contribution is provided "AS IS", without
warranties or conditions of any kind. `[COUNSEL: align disclaimer with AGPLv3
